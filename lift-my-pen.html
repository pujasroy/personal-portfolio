i lift my pen

| poetry

i lift my pen

and wander to another world

where there's no image

just words.

i then pen down some thoughts

hoping to fill a page or two

but when my mind returns

i realize how it's nothing new.

i keep writing anyway

until the thought hits me again

of how a person in the past

might've said the same things

which I now am scribbling

to make it all make sense.

so now when I pause,

i try to look through the cracks

and discover the undiscovered

which got buried under the thought.

i wave my hand at the thought

which stood as an obstacle

between me and my words

me and my love.
