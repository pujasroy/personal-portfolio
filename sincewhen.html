Since when did we start romanticizing

| poetry

Since when did we start romanticizing this massive westernisation?

Was it the point when we started replacing our hands and fingers

With spoons and forks?

Or was it the point that we started giving preferences to foreign

Languages over our own?

Was it the point that our women started believing in showing skin

To be a sign of modernity?

Or was it that point when our men began to hide behind the shadows

Of their forefathers?

Was it the mentality that gaslighted us into thinking that body hair

Is abnormal?

Or was it that which normalised cosmetic surgeries to fit into the

Beauty standards?

Was it the four cornered storeys that hindered our thinking?

Or was the inadequacy of fresh air that retarded our progress?

Was it the invasion, or was it the insecurity preceding that invasion?

At what point did losing our authenticity become fundamental to

Embracing the western elegance?
