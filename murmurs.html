murmurs

| poetry

i always dreaded the night you would finally leave, and set me free,

from your shackles of sweet delight.

in a world where no one else has claimed me as theirs, like you have,

i feared of the morning i would have to make breakfast for myself,

knowing that your jejune thoughts, or well built physique,

won't make it to the side of my chair this morning.

i doubted if i'd ever be alright gulping down the food,

that may have seemed just as tasteless as our last few convos.

though i'll admit here and now that the process of untangling

the taste of your food from my mouth,

was exponentially harder than it used to be for us

to untie our tied tongues, in the bedroom.

yet, I am here today,

bearing the thought of a future without you,

holding up despite numerous breakdowns

ever since you disappeared from my world.

with my young, reckless self gazing at the shadowy trail,

that's been wearing away with you,

leaving behind the distant murmurs...

of a beguiling future together.
