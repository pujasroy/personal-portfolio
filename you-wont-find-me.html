You won't find me

| poetry

You won't find me in the books you read,

Your love is nowhere near mine.

You won't lick me off the food you eat,

Your taste buds don't detect fine wine.

You won't relate me to the music you listen,

Your playlist doesn't qualify as divine.

You won't see a character and be "oh, she's just like her!"

Your eyes fell shut before they could see me shine.

You won't find the same person twice, not even in

the same person.
